Meet the Founders

Hessa Alkhalifa & Vicky Andrews

Hessa and Vicky, who both share a passion for health, lifestyle and sports, founded HV Holistic Health in order to create an integrated health centre with a focus on holistic principles.

A competitive sports person from an early age, Hessa is a keen runner and has completed Triathlon and Ironman events. She is a qualified CHEK Exercise Coach and a CHEK Holistic Lifestyle Coach and is passionate about guiding people to better health and nutrition, to better recovery from sports injuries and to improved physical function and flexibility.
From an early age, Vicky has dedicated herself to a career in sports and fitness. Her experience started within the British Army as a Personal Training Instructor where she developed significant skills related to the underlying disciplines of fitness, with a particular emphasis on stamina, endurance and core strength training.
